Easter Egg

What is it?

Instagram hides an Easter egg behind the settings menu. When you drag the menu down (which you don't normally do), you'll see a series of emojis leading to a present, and when you release your finger, ta-dah! You just unlocked a hidden feature allowing you to change the app's icon on your phone.

Why I like it

Firstly, it's unique. As far as I know, no other apps have ever introduced this kind of feature. For this special occasion, Instagram did an excellent job!

Secondly, it's unconventional. Not only this feature but also the way it's unlocked is pretty unconventional. Enabling this feature requires an unusual gesture: pulling down the settings menu page. Usually, we only use this kind of gesture on a mobile browser app to reload a webpage. On top of that, the travel of that pulling gesture is much longer than usual too.

Last but not least, it's fun. Who wouldn't want to try some retro versions of the app icon? It creates quite a pleasant experience, especially for advanced users who have been using this app since the beginning.
