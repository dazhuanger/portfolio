About my portfolio

At first, I used Wix to build my first portfolio. It cost me 150 bucks for a year, and didn't look good at all. There were a lot of restrictions and limitations. Not all widgets were customizable, and the responsiveness for that website was just awful.

So, I made up my mind last summer and decided to build my portfolio from scratch on my own. It was a bold decision for me since I didn't know anything about frontend development, but I am glad that I gave myself a little nudge. I took two online courses about frontend dev over the summer of 2019, designed and coded this new version of my portfolio. It's hosted on Github.
