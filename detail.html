Detail

God is in the details, so is the Devil...

I'm fascinated by micro-interactions, so I borrowed Kobe's TV show's name -- Detail, to name this section of my portfolio. I want to share my thoughts on some good and bad micro-interactions that I have seen in apps, websites and other products.

As a big fan of data-driven UX research, I wish I had enough data to support my comments and recommendations on these products. Unfortunately, they are all based on a designer's instinct, which could be a good thing to have when comprehensive research data is not available.
